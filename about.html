About Us

What Are We

By saving abandoned dogs and providing them with a fulfilling life in the community, we hope to affect societal change. We are working to provide a sustainable and humane habitat for animals. Yet until every animal feels secure, revered, and loved, we all have a lot of difficult work ahead of us.

What We do

Via our rescue programme, which deals with circumstances where the animal needs round-the-clock care, we save street animals. We react to calls on our helpline every day from people asking us to assist animals hurt after being hit by a car, as well as those with severe injuries, illnesses, burns, etc.

Most Indian cities lack a helpline, a hospital, or a sanctuary for plighting animals. Animals are forced to endure in those situations alone and may even die slowly and painfully
